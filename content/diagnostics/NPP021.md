### NPP021 Filler missing X
To make a `FILLER` an actual filler and not a variable name, it has to have a length followed by an `X`.

## Invalid

```natural
1 #VAR (A50)
1 REDEFINE #VAR
2 FILLER 10
2 #REST (A40)
```

## Valid

```natural
1 #VAR (A50)
1 REDEFINE #VAR
2 FILLER 10X
2 #REST (A40)
```

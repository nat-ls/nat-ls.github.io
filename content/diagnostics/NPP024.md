### NPP024 Target of REDEFINE can't contain DYNAMIC
A redefinition can't contain a variable with `DYNAMIC` length.

## Invalid

```natural
1 #VAR (A50)
1 REDEFINE #VAR
2 #REST (A) DYNAMIC /* DYNAMIC not allowed here
```
